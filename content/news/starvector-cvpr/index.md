---
title: StarVector accepted at CVPR 2025
date: 2025-03-01
---
Our paper "StarVector: Generating Scalable Vector Graphics Code from Images" has been accepted at CVPR 2025. Former intern Juan A. Rodriguez co-founded [QuiverAI](https://quiver.ai) based on this research, raising an $8.3M seed round led by Andreessen Horowitz.
