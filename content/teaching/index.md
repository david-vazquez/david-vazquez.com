## Current Teaching

### Machine Learning for Computer Vision
**Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona** | Master's in Computer Vision and AI | 2013 to Present

Graduate level course covering deep learning methods for visual recognition, object detection, semantic segmentation, and generative models. Taught annually as part of one of Europe's leading graduate programs in computer vision, jointly run by UAB and the Computer Vision Center.

### Graduate Student Supervision
**Polytechnique Montréal / MILA** | 2025 to Present

Co supervising doctoral students in multimodal AI and AI agents, in collaboration with Christopher Pal and other MILA affiliated professors.

## Program Development

Co designed the Inter University Master's in Computer Vision and AI curriculum in Barcelona during my postdoctoral years (2015 to 2017), collaborating with Prof. Maria Vanrell to integrate deep learning and modern AI techniques. Recruited professors from the Montreal AI ecosystem to develop course materials and deliver lectures. Helped position the program at the forefront of AI education in Europe.
