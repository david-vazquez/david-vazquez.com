Initiated and led the development of an experimental autonomous vehicle platform at the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona. Designed the full perception system (object detection, semantic segmentation, 3D reconstruction, SLAM), secured partnerships with Tazzari (vehicle integration) and NVIDIA (prototype hardware), and supervised multidisciplinary teams across perception, planning, vehicle control, and GPU accelerated vision algorithms.
