abstract: 'Health professionals use 2D ultrasound images to measure abdominal muscles for diagnosing and treating low back pain, but interpretation is difficult and requires specialized training. This paper presents a deep learning approach using a modified Fully Convolutional Network architecture to automate abdominal muscle thickness measurements by localizing measurement endpoints. The method achieves a Mean Absolute Error of 0.3125 on the TrA400 dataset, comparable to skilled technicians, reducing observer variability and improving clinical outcomes.'
